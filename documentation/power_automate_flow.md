# Power Automate — Finance Variance Alert

## Overview
Automated daily alert that notifies the Finance team 
when revenue variance requires review.

## Trigger
Scheduled: Every day at 10:00 AM

## Action
Send email via Office 365 Outlook to Finance team.

## Alert Logic
Runs daily after pipeline refresh to notify Finance
of any revenue reconciliation results requiring attention.

## Recipients
Finance Team and Analytics Lead

## Business Value
Eliminates manual monitoring of variance reports.
Finance team receives proactive notification instead
of reactively checking dashboards.
